Mikayla Miklos | 3 website Ideas

Website Idea 1 | Personal journal

My first idea for a website is a personal journal logging the work I get done throughout the week.
This could include photos of my work done that week or a checklist of things I need to do. Basically
it'd be a blog that details my schoolwork in an cute but concise manor.

Website Idea 2 | Style Blog

My second idea and the one that appeals to me the most, is creating a website/blog that documents my personal style.
This could range from my favorite fashion, music, or photos that appeal to me at the moment. I want this site to
have a more personal feel and be something fun to mess around with, sort of like a Tumblr blog.

Website Idea 3 | Womens travel

Lastly I think a travel website aimed towards women could be cool. It would have information on safety and tips
for women who want to solo travel. For example I could have an interactive map with the least and most friendly countries
for women to travel to. It would have an informational tone and be fairly professional.
