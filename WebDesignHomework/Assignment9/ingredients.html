    • Ingredients you will need to make Deviled Eggs:


  • EGGS
  • MAYONAISE
  • MUSTARD
  • SALT & PEPPER
  • PAPRIKA

    • In this recipe you will have to hard boil the eggs. Dijon mustard is recommended but not required.
    The ammount of salt, pepper, and paprika added is up to personal preference. We are making the basic
    deviled eggs so we will be using paprika, but you can mix it up and add other garnishes. Some other garnishes you can add
    could be chives, bacon, siracha, smoked salmon, cheese, and so much more! It's all up to your personal preferance.



    Amount Needed

    EGGS MAYO MUSTARD SALT PEPPER PAPRIKA
    12 1/3 CUP 1 1/2 TSP PREFERENCE PREFERENCE GARNISH
