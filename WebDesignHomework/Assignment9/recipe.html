    • Lets Put it All Together!

    The photo above is of some deviled eggs my sister and I made together! We took a
    photo because I messed and put way too much paprika on top of one of them on haha.

    1. 1.) Hard boil eggs: using your preffered method hard boil 12 eggs.
      Normally because I'm lazy I go to the store and buy eggs that have already been hard boiled for me.


    2. 2.) Peel and Cut the Eggs: once you've boiled the eggs peel the shells and
      cut the eggs in half


    3. 3.) Filling: Remove the yolks from the halves and place them in a bowl.
      Mash them up then add the mayo, mustard, salt, and pepper. Then mix it all together.


    4. 4.) Assemble: Use a tool of your choice, like a spoon or piping bag. Put the filling back
      into the egg white. Then garnish with paprika.


    5. 5.) Enjoy! Taste your delicious Deviled Eggs.
